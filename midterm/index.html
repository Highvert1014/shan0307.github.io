The Evolution of the NBA

The National Basketball Association (NBA) is the premier professional basketball league in the world. Founded in 1946 as the Basketball Association of America (BAA), it merged with the National Basketball League (NBL) in 1949 to form what we now know as the NBA. Over the decades, the league has transformed dramatically, introducing new rules, expanding teams, and fostering global superstars.

NBA Timeline: Key Milestones

  • June 6, 1946: BAA is founded in New York City.
  • November 1, 1946: First BAA game played; New York defeats Toronto 68-66.
  • 1947: Philadelphia Warriors win first BAA Championship.
  • August 3, 1949: BAA and NBL merge to form the NBA.
  • 1950s-2023: The NBA evolves with rule changes, global expansion, and iconic moments.
