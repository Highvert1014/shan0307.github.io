The Evolution of the NBA

The National Basketball Association (NBA) is the premier professional basketball league in the world. Founded in 1946 as the Basketball Association of America (BAA), it merged with the National Basketball League (NBL) in 1949 to form what we now know as the NBA.

NBA Timeline: Key Milestones

  • June 6, 1946: BAA is founded in New York City.
  • November 1, 1946: First BAA game played.
  • 1947: Philadelphia Warriors win first BAA Championship.
  • August 3, 1949: BAA and NBL merge to form the NBA.
